Opobo Marathon

The Opobo Marathon is an annual marathon race started in 2019. The Marathon is held every first week of January in Opobo Kingdom, Opobo/Nkoro Local Government Area of Rivers State Nigeria.
See More

First Week of January

Location

4 years in a row, Opobo Marathon become an experience to look out for runners and marathon enthusiasts in the South-south of Nigeria.
